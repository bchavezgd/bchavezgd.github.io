About Me

I’m Brian Chavez. A graphic designer according to my school. I don’t stop there. I’m always looking at every kind of design to gather inspiration. My career path started with me wanting to be a mechanical engineer, until I realized that I can have a greater impact on the world through good design. It stems from me being the kid that would take household items apart just to put them back together. I needed to know how things worked, and eventually, I became passionate about improving whatever I took apart. From my second passion of bicycles to making bad industrial design sketches, I’m always making up problems that I can fix, biting off more than I can chew, and having a great time doing it.
Brian Chavez, Graphic Designer, weightlifter, buddhist.
